Synesthesia

The production of a sense impression relating to one sense or part of the body
by stimulation of another sense or part of the body.

About this project

"This project is about make a invisible thing to visible, so I created a website that people can experience symptom of synesthesia. Synesthesia is a condition in which one sense is simultaneously perceived as if by one or more additional senses. This website will especially guide through color and auditory sense."
